  

## CU Assassins

### 10 Mar 2013

[![](/img/projects/cu-assassins.png)](http://assassins.columbiaesc.com/)

By Robert Ying

Ever tried to organize a game of Assassins? It's much harder than you think, especially when you get more people involved. CU Assassins makes it possible for anybody to start up their own game, complete with teams, police, disavowal, and notifications.
